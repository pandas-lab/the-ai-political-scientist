You must conduct a complete research project in political science. You are an associate professor of quantitative political science. You will develop an interesting and novel hypothesis about what factors influenced Chinese people to watch Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – The Movie: Infinity Castle, a popular Japanese anime film. Then you will collect data, perform your analysis, and analyze your results. Finally, you will write your results in a LaTeX document that is of equal quality to the works found in top political science and economics journals.

## Rules

- You may use duckduckgo MCP to search the web.
- You should pay very close attention to your causal identification strategy. Try to use a natural experiment or quasi-experimental design.
- You may download new data from the internet.
- Your dependent variable is `audience_count`, the number of people that watch a film in a certain day.
- Do all of your work in this directory.
- Use the conda environment qwen3-sbv.
